Important information for users on the appearance of the Corona virus (COVID-19)

As a responsible company, we take all necessary measures to protect the health and safety of our employees and their families, as well as our customers and business partners.

We perform all our business activities in accordance with all preventive measures for protection against the spread of viruses and the state of emergency introduced by the Government of the Republic of Serbia, and a specially formed team of our colleagues constantly monitors the development of the situation. We have taken additional preventive measures and ensured that throughout the state of emergency and in the event of a continuous spread of the virus, all processes in the company, which are crucial for users, function smoothly.

Guided by this idea, we went a step further and introduced the hashtag #StayHomeHouse through which we appealed to all our users to take care of themselves and others. You can still do everything online.

On this page you can find all the important information and recommendations regarding our services, network and measures that we apply every day.

We are at your disposal for any help and support.

You can contact us at any time via the Vip customer service at 060/1234, as well as via the website and our social networks.

Caring for employees

As part of preventive measures, already during the spread of the virus in Europe, Vip mobile introduced work from home, reduction of travel abroad, as well as disinfectants for workplaces, protection and instructions on how to use it all, in order to have safe work. Meetings are held via video conferencing, telephone or through any other alternative channel.

Our crisis team meets daily and assesses the situation on the basis of which it takes preventive measures to ensure that, in the event of further spread of the virus, employees are adequately protected, and at the same time ensure the continuity of our business.

Customer care

We know how important it is in this situation to be available and in contact with everyone, so we have provided all our users with free use of Viber, Facebook, Facebook Messenger and WhatsApp via Net for Chat tariff add-on.
