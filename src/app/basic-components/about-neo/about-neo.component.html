Why are we different?

The largest greenfield investment in Serbia ever

So far, we have invested almost a billion euros in our network, opening Vip centers throughout Serbia and educating employees.

We are part of the A1 Telekom Austria Group, which allows us to share experience, knowledge and expertise.

We improve the market in the interest of users

We were the first to enable complete freedom of communication by introducing UNLIMITED minutes and messages to all networks. There is no charge for number activation and call set-up in our network, and calls to customer service agents are free. We are the only ones who allow users to test the Vip network completely free of charge for a month via Come and try tariffs, without any obligations or costs.

Gender equality and commitment to the family

In our country, women and men are equally represented in all positions. The conditions for advancement, earnings and training are the same for everyone.

We are proud of the fact that we have provided employees, and especially parents, with a good balance between business and private life. That is why we were the first in Serbia to receive the Family Friendly Enterprise certificate.

And as we believe that every mother should carelessly welcome her newborn, we pay future mothers in Vip the difference up to the agreed salary. All Vipovci for the third child receive a monthly compensation of 10,000 dinars until the child is 15 years old.

We care about the community

We initiate and support numerous activities aimed at improving the quality of life of people with autism and disability. We educate children, parents and the elderly and help them step into the digital world through the Safe Kids and Digital Classrooms projects.
